Blogging again

July 21, 2026

After many years without posting something publicly, I am back at it again. This is the nth attempt to have a blog, but this time it’ll be different (famous last words).

Why?

Because I have found that writing is the best way for me to learn, check my assumptions and ensure I am not mixing things up. Most importantly, I am not striving for perfection, which was a huge blocker in previous attempts, nor I am writing for a specific audience. If any Internet stranger stumbles upon these notes, and find them useful, that’s great, and I encourage you to reach out if you have feedback – my email address is in my CV.

Reading folks who are consistent at blogging gave me the motivation to do it myself. Some of them are: Dan Luu, Simon Willison, Murat Demibas, and Jordan Gonen. There are more, but those are the ones on top of my head.

I don’t have much else to say other than: let’s do this!
